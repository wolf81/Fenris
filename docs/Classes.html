  • ServiceLocator

    The ServiceLocator can be used to register and retrieve various services. Use the service locator to prevent creating a lot of singletons in your codebase. Instead register classes for global use in the service locator and pass the service locator around as a dependency. That way all registered services will stay easily testable.
